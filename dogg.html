Frequently Asked Questions

$SYNR Yields

Every Weed Farm comes with a daily Play & Earn revenue and $SYNR income should be collected in a daily basis.

What can I do with $SYNR?

$SYNR is the native currency of Mobland ecosystem. Players can exchange their $SYNR to USDT via Kucoin or trade them for in-game items (NFTs) to further develop their mob and farms.

How do limits work?

Each player is limited to have one type of digital weed farm. In order to choose another one, players will first have to sell their existing digital weed farm to another player via in-game marketplace. Choose wisely and upgrade your existing farm by maintaining it.

I am late for the sale, next wen?

We have no plans to add more Digital Weed Farms into Mobland ecosystem. You can check in-game market if any other player is selling their farms. We are working on plannings to deliver more Play & Earn mechanics into our game in the future, keep an eye on our official Twitter and Discord announcements.
